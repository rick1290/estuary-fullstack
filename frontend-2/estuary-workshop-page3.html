Overview

Your dreams are not noise — they are cartography. This workshop teaches you to read the map.

The Dream Cartography Workshop is a guided, in-person group experience that reframes how you relate to your nighttime mind. Rather than treating dreams as random noise or mysterious omens, we approach them as living documents — full of symbol, pattern, and personal meaning waiting to be decoded.

Over the course of this single immersive session, Shaman Dave leads participants through a structured process of active dream recall, symbol mapping, and group sharing. No prior experience with dream work is required — only a willingness to look inward.
